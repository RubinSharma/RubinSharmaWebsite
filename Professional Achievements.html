Now, in this section we are going to talk about my professional experiences.

I never had to work while I was in school for secondary education and at college for my higher secondary education. Truly my professional journey began after I moved to Australia when I had to work inorder to complete my basic expenses and my college fee. I would be studying full-time at TAFE as well as other times at home to make sure I actually understand what I learned in college.
Making sure to balance my studies, work, and time for myself was a massive challange which I somewhat was able to overcome.

After moving to Australia in 2018, I was very naive. I was unsure about a lot of thing but one thing I knew for sure was that I had to keep going and keep grinding. I am going to be very brief about what sort of jobs I did in my earlier days.

Cleaner

Later, I started working as a Cleaner. Even though the job required low cognitive and problem solving ability, I was able to meet a lot of interesting people which was a positive aspect of the job.

Housekeeping
